The picture shown on the right is Santo Domingo. Santo domingo is the capital of the Dominican Republic. Santo Domingo is also is the oldest European-founded city in Americas.

The picture shown on the right is Pico Duarte. Pico Duarte is the tallest mountain in the caribbean at 10,164 feet high. It is also located in the Cordillera Central mountain range in the Dominican Republic.

The picture shown on the right is Lake Enriquillo and it is the largest lake in the caribbean. It is located on the southwestern part of the Dominican Republic. This lake is special because it is below sea level and it is a saltwater lake, while most lakes are freshwater.
