The name of the resort shown in the picture to the right is called the Hard Rock Hotel & Casino which is located in Punta Cana. This resort stands out from others because it offers an all-inclusive experience and it's adult- and kid-friendly. The Hard Rock & Casino is mostly known for being a huge resort and offering a lot of entertainment. This resort offers 13 different pools which include kid attractions such as kid pools, and water slides. However, they also include adult-only pools that offer unlimited alcoholic beverages as part of the all-inclusive package. They also offer a lazy river.

Nickelodeon Hotels & Resorts located in Punta Cana is also another adult and kid-friendly resort. However, travelers seem to find that this resort is more kid-oriented than adult-oriented. This is a great resort for kids because they get the chance to take pictures with their favorite Nickelodeon characters. Rooms and suites are also decorated with Nickelodeon charcters like Spongebob, Dora, and the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.
