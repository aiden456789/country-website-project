The dish in the picture is called chicharron con tostones and it is a popular dominican dish. chicharron is pork that is seasoned and fried until its crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside. While tostones are basically sliced plantains that are also seasoned and fried until crispy on the outside.

The dish in the picture is called mangu and it is a popular dominican dish which is usually eaten for breakfast. In the picture, it is paired with fried salami, fried cheese, a sunny-side-up egg, and pickled red onions. Mangu is made of boiled, mashed green plantains, and salt.

The dish in the picture is called empanadas. Empanadas are made from a dough with seasoned meat, chicken, or cheese inside. Empanadas are crispy on the outside and warm and hot on the inside. Some prefer to eat it with a mayo and ketchup sauce, or hot sauce.

The dish in the picture is rice and beans. Rice and beans is a staple to the Dominican culture because it is often eaten as a side of every meal. It is mostly eaten with meats like chicken, beef, or pork.
