The dish in the picture is called chicharron con tostones and it is a popular dominican dish. chicharron is basically pork that is seasoned and fried until its crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside. While tostones are basically sliced plantains that are also seasoned and fried until crispy on the outside. These 2 foods pair together well because they are both crunchy and savory.

The dish in the picture is called mangu and it is a popular dominican dish which is usually eaten for breakfast. In the picture, it is paired with fried salami, fried cheese, a sunny-side-up egg, and pickled red onions. Mangu is made of boiled, mashed green plantains, and salt. Mangu is a staple in the Dominican Republic because of its history, affordability, and culture.
